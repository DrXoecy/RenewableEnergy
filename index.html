What is it?

Much like a renewable resource, renewable energy comes from a source that has an infinite supply - it will never end. More specifically, renewable energy is less harmful to the environment and does not release as many greenhouse gases. There are many different kinds of renewable energy and these all range in difficulty to harness and the concentration of the power. This means that only certain energy sources are worth thinking about as the results of all the painstaking effort could be rather minute.

Examples

There are many different sources of energy in the world around us. Some of these are more rewarding than others and some have extremely negative impacts on the environment. Therefore, only certain types of energy can be classed as renewable. Some examples of this are:

  • Wind

  • Solar

  • Hydropower

  • Geothermal Energy

  • Tidal Energy

All of these different types of energy are renewable and efficient. We can also see more and more of them being implemented each day.
